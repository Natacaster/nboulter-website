WHAT I HELP WITH

Start with one project or build a fuller retention system. Either way, the goal is simple: better messaging, stronger customer journeys, and more revenue from the people already in your world.

Email Campaigns

Strategic campaign emails built to support launches, promotions, product drops, seasonal pushes, and everyday revenue generation without sounding pushy or disconnected from your brand.

Lifecycle Flows

Welcome flows, abandoned cart emails, browse abandonment, post-purchase sequences, win-back flows, and other key automations that help convert new subscribers and bring customers back.

Retention Strategy

Messaging support that helps your brand keep customers engaged after the first purchase so you are not depending only on new traffic and ad spend to grow.

Landing Page Copy

Clear, conversion-focused copy for landing pages that support offers, promotions, product education, and brand trust so your traffic has somewhere stronger to go.

SMS

Text marketing that feels timely, readable, and on-brand while supporting launches, reminders, promotions, and repeat purchases.

Funnels and Offer Messaging

If your customer journey feels disconnected, I can help shape the messaging across your funnel so each step feels more intentional, more cohesive, and more likely to convert.

How It Works

  1. Book a call: We talk through your brand, your current email setup, your customer journey, and where revenue may be getting left on the table.
  2. Create a plan: We map out the emails, flows, campaigns, pages, or funnel support that makes the most sense for your goals. Every brand is different, so every scope is custom.
  3. Write and refine: I build the copy around your brand voice, customer behavior, and conversion goals so the messaging feels aligned and usable.
  4. Launch and optimize: We get it live, look at performance, and keep improving what helps drive more opens, clicks, purchases, and repeat customers.
