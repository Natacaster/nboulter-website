Email vs Funnels vs Sales Pages

Not sure what you actually need? Here’s a simple breakdown so you can see where each piece fits and why it matters for your business growth.

Service What It Does Why It Matters
Email Marketing Keeps your audience warm, builds trust, and quietly sells in the background If you’re tired of shouting on Instagram every day, this is your “finally breathe” system. Emails do the heavy lifting for you while you live your life. It’s the easiest way to turn followers into paying clients.
Funnel Copy Creates a simple, step-by-step path that guides someone from “hmm, interesting” to “okay, I’m in” Funnels are for when you want things to just… flow. It’s the map that turns strangers into buyers without you babysitting every step.
Sales Page Copy Shows your offer in its best light, clearly, confidently, and without the hypey nonsense You need this when you’re selling a program or package that deserves a real explanation, not just a cute caption. A great sales page makes people feel seen and ready to take action (even if they came in cold).
